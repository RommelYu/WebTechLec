Steps on how to open our website:

Step 1: Open the '9358A-G7' folder
Step 2: Open the 'website' folder
Step 3: Look for the 'index.html' file and double click it. Once you double click the file it will run on your default browser.
Step 4: You will now see the home page of our website. Click on the "MIDTERM TOPICS" located in the middle part of the webpage 
        to see the midterm topic lists. In order for the students to see our website midterm lecture notes, just drag the mouse
        pointer on the 'LECTURE' tab located at the upper left corner of the website and click 'MIDTERMS'. There you will 
        see the different topics in midterms in Web Systems and Technologies.
